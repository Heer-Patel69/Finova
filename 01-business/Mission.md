# Finova - Mission & Core Pillars

## Mission Statement
Empower students worldwide to effortlessly track every transaction, understand their financial behavior, and save more through AI-powered coaching, automation, and engaging daily habits.

---

## Core Pillars

### 1. Track Everything
Users should be able to track every aspect of their financial life:
- **Income**: Salary, Scholarship, Pocket Money, Freelancing, Gifts, Refunds, Other.
- **Expenses**: Food, Groceries, Coffee, Shopping, Rent, Transport (Metro, Taxi), Entertainment, Education, Medical, Utilities, Travel, Other.
- **Bills**: Internet, Mobile, Electricity, Water, Netflix, Spotify, Rent, Insurance.
- **Wallets**: Cash, Debit Card, Credit Card, Bank Accounts, Digital Wallets (Unlimited wallets supported).

### 2. Understand Everything
AI analyzes spending:
- Spending trends
- Category comparison
- Merchant analysis
- Weekly & Monthly insights
- Financial health
- Cash flow
- Saving rate
- Budget utilization

### 3. Improve Everything
AI becomes a financial coach:
- Safe daily spending
- Budget optimization
- Saving recommendations
- Goal planning
- Spending prediction
- Risk alerts
- Financial summaries
