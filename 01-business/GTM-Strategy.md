# Finova - Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Version 1.0

This plan covers the customer acquisition, activation, and product launch strategy for **Finova**.

---

## 1. Initial Launch Focus: The Georgia Cohort (First 50 Students)

To build a high-retention user base and iterate quickly on feedback, Finova will launch exclusively to an initial group of **50 university students in Tbilisi, Georgia**.

### 1.1 Target Demographics
- International students enrolled in local universities (e.g., Tbilisi State University, Caucasus University).
- Students navigating local currency (GEL) alongside base currencies (USD, INR, EUR) for allowances.

### 1.2 Acquisition Channels
- **Campus Ambassadors**: Enlist 3 student leaders at TSU/Caucasus University to share invite codes in course chats.
- **Student Group Partnerships**: Engage directly with international student associations.
- **Word-of-Mouth via Finova Split**: By using group splits for rent/meals, active users invite classmates naturally to settle debts.

---

## 2. Retention & Habit Formation Playbook

- **Day 1 (Activation)**: Complete Onboarding in under 2 minutes; set first Savings Goal (e.g., "Phone").
- **Day 3 (Streak Lock)**: Receive first push notification prompting a "No Spend Day" challenge.
- **Day 7 (AI Review)**: Display first automated weekly summary outlining money saved compared to the prior week.
- **Day 30 (Referral Loop)**: Prompt active users on high streaks to share invite links to unlock exclusive Pro badges.
