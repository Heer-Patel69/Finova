# Finova - Business & Monetization Model

## Version 1.0

Finova is designed with a student-centric freemium model. Since students are budget-conscious, monetization is driven primarily by referral commissions and premium B2B partnerships rather than aggressive user subscription fees.

---

## 1. Monetization Streams

### 1.1 Freemium Subscription ("Finova Pro")
- **Free Tier**: Includes manual expense logging, basic multi-currency support, 3 active wallets, and standard AI chat queries (up to 5 per day).
- **Pro Tier ($1.99/month)**:
  - Unlimited wallets.
  - OCR receipt scanning (Phase 5).
  - Unlimited context-aware AI Coach queries and proactive cash flow forecasting.
  - Custom category creation and priority local leaderboard rank.

### 1.2 Merchant Commission (Local Student Deals)
- **Concept**: Integrate with local businesses (cafes, transit, bookstores, student housings) to present context-aware discounts.
- **Workflow**:
  - AI identifies spending patterns (e.g. "You spent $40 on transport this month").
  - AI offers a deal: "Get a 20% discount on a monthly Metro card through our partner."
  - Finova collects a referral fee on each redeemed voucher or transaction.

### 1.3 University B2B Sponsorships
- **Concept**: Partner with universities to provide Finova Pro to their student body as a financial literacy resource.
- **Value Proposition**: Universities buy bulk licenses to support international student onboarding and reduce financial stress in their campus populations.

---

## 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) vs. Lifetime Value (LTV)

- **Target CAC**: < $1.50 per user (driven by organic viral loops from **Finova Split** groups and word-of-mouth on campus).
- **Target LTV**: $18.00 (derived from average Pro subscription durations and partner deal redemption margins).
