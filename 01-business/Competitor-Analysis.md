## 2. Competitive Edge & Positioning

### 2.1 The "Passive" Tracker Gap
Traditional finance apps require users to manually catalog expenses retrospectively. This creates friction and leads to users abandoning the app. Finova shifts the paradigm by offering **gamified streak challenges** (encouraging daily check-ins) and providing **conversational advice** ("Can I buy this?") before transactions happen.

### 2.2 Socioeconomic Sensitivity in Gamification
Other banking apps reward users based on account sizes or points accumulated from high spending. Finova's leaderboards rank users strictly on **financial habits** (streaks, quest completion, and staying under budget) to build a friendly, inclusive environment for all student income groups.
